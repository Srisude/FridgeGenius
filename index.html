Key Features

Recipe Suggestions

Discover delicious recipes based on the ingredients you already have in your fridge.

Smart Shopping Lists

Create shopping lists automatically based on what you need to cook your favorite meals.

Food Management

Easily track your food items to minimize waste and make the most of your groceries.

How It Works

  1. Registration: Download the app and register your food items.
  2. Add Items: Snap photos of products; the app uses AI for recognition.
  3. Find Recipes: Get recipe ideas based on available ingredients.
  4. Track Usage: Update your fridge list as you cook and enjoy meals.
  5. Generate Lists: Receive smart shopping lists for future needs.

Benefits

Fridge Genius not only helps you manage your time and health but also promotes a sustainable lifestyle. It's a practical and user-friendly tool designed for everyone!
